Last updated: August 16, 2024

Introduction

Evan Levine Real Estate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Our app may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number when you choose to provide it
  • NFC Data: Information read from NFC business cards (with your permission)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app and services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide real estate services and information
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Improve our app and services
  • Send you relevant real estate updates (with your consent)

NFC Technology

Our app uses Near Field Communication (NFC) technology to read business card information. This feature:

  • Only works when you actively tap your device to an NFC tag
  • Requires your explicit permission each time
  • Only reads information you choose to scan

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to provide our services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
